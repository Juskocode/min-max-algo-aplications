How it works: Minimax & Heuristics

Minimax Algorithm: The AI simulates thousands of future game states (up to 5 moves ahead) to find the path that guarantees the best outcome, assuming the opponent also plays optimally.

Heuristics: Since looking at every possible move until the end is impossible, the AI uses a scoring system (weights) to evaluate positions:

  • Center Advantage: Placing pieces in the middle columns offers more winning connections.
  • Line Potential: Sets of 2 or 3 pieces that can become 4 are highly valued.
  • Defensive Play: The AI prioritizes blocking your winning lines.

The numbers above the board show the AI's score for each move. Higher is better!
